The .PNG Format

About This Image

This photograph displays the remains of the Franis Scott Key Bridge. The image was taken on 3/30/24.

Why PNG Format?

While not ideal for photographers. PNGS hold all of the data in a lossless compression format, meaning you can edit it multiple times without losing any quality. You get nice perks with PNG, such as support for transparency and 24 bit colors.
